# Alert History

![Alert History](../../media/nauticsafe/histoalertes.png)

The 3rd tab presents active alarms and the history of alarms on the boat.

By clicking on an alarm, you can access the details. Your dealer can signal here that they have taken charge of the alert. It will be "closed" when the situation returns to normal.

![Taken Charge](../../media/nauticsafe/priseencharge.png)
