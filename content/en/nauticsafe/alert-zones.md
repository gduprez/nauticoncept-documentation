# Alert and Attachment Zones

![Zones](../../media/nauticsafe/zones.png)

The last tab presents zone management:

- **Attachment Zones**: geographic zones in which the boat must be located when you are not on board
- **Alert Zones**: zones for which an alert must be triggered if the boat enters them

## Define a Zone

![Zone Definition](../../media/nauticsafe/definitionzone.png)

Define zones by giving them:

- A name
- A type (attachment zone or alert zone)
- The series of points delimiting the zone
