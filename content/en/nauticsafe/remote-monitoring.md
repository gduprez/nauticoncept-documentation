# Monitoring Your Boat from Your Smartphone

![NauticSafe Boat Details](../../media/nauticsafe/fichebateau.png)

The NauticSafe module monitors vital organs on board to prevent damage and anticipate maintenance, thanks to alerts and logs.

NauticSafe offers a permanent eye on your boat, rented or lent. It minimizes risks, bad surprises, and thus secures your investment.

## Connection

- **On Board**: connect using the Bluetooth button
- **Remote**: retrieve the last recorded state and history by clicking on the status button

## Available Statuses

- **ASLEEP**: in standby
- **RUN**: engine running
- **ALARM**: in alarm
- **ONBOARD**: someone is on board
