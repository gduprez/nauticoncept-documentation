# On Board My Boat

![On Board Status](../../media/nauticsafe/status.png)

Signal your presence on board and view sensors.

The module status is indicated at the top left (for example ALARM).

## Unlock Sensors

Click on "Unlock" to deactivate the shock and heel sensors of your boat. These sensors, active when at the pontoon, allow knowing if the boat "hits" the pontoon or if someone boards (for small units).

## Real-Time Data

This screen shows sensor data in real time:

- Temperature
- Water presence
- Service voltages
- Engine
- After circuit breaker
- GPS Speed

Alerts appear in red (for example circuit breaker deactivated).
