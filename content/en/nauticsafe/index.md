# NauticSafe

The NauticSafe module monitors vital organs on board to prevent damage and anticipate maintenance, thanks to alerts and logs.

NauticSafe offers a permanent eye on your boat, rented or lent. It minimizes risks, bad surprises, and thus secures your investment.
