# Configure Alarm Thresholds

![Threshold Configuration](../../media/nauticsafe/Newconfigseuils.png)

In the Sensors tab, configure the module alerts:

- **Service Voltage**: an Alert level and an Alarm level
- **Engine Voltage**: an Alert level and an Alarm level
- **Temperature**: a low alert level, and a high alert level
- **Internal Voltage**: the module is equipped with an internal battery which allows it to continue functioning for a few days if it is no longer powered. Leave this value at 2.8V

> Do not forget to click on the "Save" button at the top right.
