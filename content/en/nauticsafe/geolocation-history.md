# Geolocation History

![History Map](../../media/nauticsafe/histocarte.png)

The Map tab allows viewing your boat's position history.

NauticSafe uses the American GPS system to locate itself. Precision is +/- 10 meters, especially when the boat is not moving. Precision can be weakened if the boat is stored inside a building, or if it is made of metal.

## Usage

- Select the desired period
- Zoom to discover details
- The color of points represents the module status (red = ALARM)
- Click on a point to know the precise status, any alarm, and the precise time
