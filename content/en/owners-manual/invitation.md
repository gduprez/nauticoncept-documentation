# Invitation

![Email Invitation](../../media/manuel-proprietaires/nauticoncept_mail_invitation.png)

Your dealer has sent you an invitation to use the NAUTICONCEPT application. You have received an email containing your login credentials.

The credentials include your email address and a temporary password necessary for the first connection. The mail also contains download links for iPhone/iPad and Android.
