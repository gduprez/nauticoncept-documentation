# Take a Photo

![Take a Photo](../../media/manuel-proprietaires/nauticoncept_newphoto-1.png)

Click on the camera icon to access three options:

## Photo

Approx. 50% compression, optimized for internet, fast access. Suitable for overview shots.

## HD Photo

Maximum resolution of the phone, longer loading, excellent detail. Ideal for serial numbers and documents.

## Photo from Gallery

Imports an existing photo from the smartphone or tablet.

> After importation, access properties to perform classification by tags.
