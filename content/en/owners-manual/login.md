# Login

![Login Screen](../../media/manuel-proprietaires/nauticoncept_double_login.png)

After launching the application, the home screen asks you to enter your email address and the password you received. After entering them, click on identification.

The next screen prompts you to choose a four-digit PIN code for quick subsequent access. The next time you use it, only the PIN code will be necessary.
