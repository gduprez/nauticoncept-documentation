# Photo Management

![Photo Management](../../media/manuel-proprietaires/nauticoncept_photo1.png)

NAUTICONCEPT is a tool that promotes communication between all stakeholders on a boat by using the photo as a central medium.

The photo tab allows you to visualize and share photographs of the boat and its mechanical components and equipment.
