# My Team

![My Team](../../media/manuel-concessionnaires/nauticoncept_equipe.png)

The "My Team" page allows you to manage the list of your collaborators (mechanics). An add button is located at the top right of the screen.

## Add a Mechanic

You must provide the following information:

- Last Name
- First Name
- Unique email address
- Phone number

## Automatic Invitation

When you register a new technician, they receive an email invitation with a personal password to log in to the application.

## Status Management

It is possible to deactivate the account of one of your technicians using the "Status" field present on their file.
