# Photo Organization

![Photo Organization](../../media/manuel-concessionnaires/nauticoncept_photo_filtre2.png)

Photos are classified according to tags that describe them best.

The "Filter" button allows you to quickly find all photos concerning a subject (for example the engine...). These tags can be combined: it is possible to classify a photo in both "Engine" and "Serial Numbers". Thus it is easy to find all photos concerning serial numbers, but also find all photos concerning the engine.

The checkmark at the top right of the screen validates the selected filters. The list of displayed photos will be filtered accordingly.
