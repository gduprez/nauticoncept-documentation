# Home and Menu

![Dealer Home Page](../../media/manuel-concessionnaires/nauticoncept_accueil_concessionnaire.png)

The home page presents "My Fleet": the list of boats you have under maintenance. The boat is presented with a main photo, the make and model, its name, and its registration. The client's name also appears to facilitate the search.

At the bottom of the screen, tabs allow access to:

- Fleet geolocation
- Messaging

## Burger Menu

![Burger Menu](../../media/manuel-concessionnaires/menu_burger.png)

By clicking on the 3-line icon (burger menu), you access the following features:

- **My Fleet**: home page
- **Internal Messaging**
- **Alerts**: NauticSafe modules
- **My Team**: mechanic management
- **Schedule**: intervention management
- **My Account**: personal information
- **My Garage**: dealership information
- **About**: application version
- **Log Out**: session closure
