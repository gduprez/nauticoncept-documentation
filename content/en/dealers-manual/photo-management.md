# Photo Management

![Photo Management](../../media/manuel-concessionnaires/nauticoncept_photo1.png)

NAUTICONCEPT is a tool that promotes communication between all stakeholders on a boat by using the photo as a central medium. The photo tab allows you to view and share photos of your boat and all its mechanical components and equipment.
