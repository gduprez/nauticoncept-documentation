# Schedule and Interventions

![Schedule and Interventions](../../media/manuel-concessionnaires/nauticoncept_interventions.png)

From the Burger menu, the "Schedule" link allows access to the schedule and the entire history of interventions made on each boat, by each mechanic.

## Display Options

A screen invites you to choose the schedule you wish to display:

- **My Schedule**: schedule of your performed and planned interventions
- **By Boat**: interventions for a boat
- **By Mechanic**: for one of your mechanics
- **Global**: groups all schedules into a single page

## Creating Interventions

A **+** button at the top right of the screen allows you to plan new interventions. Interventions contain:

- A subject
- A start date/time
- An end date/time
- A description
- An assigned mechanic

## Intervention Statuses

The status of the intervention determines the progress on this intervention. By default, it will be set to "New". The different possible statuses are:

- **New**: intervention requested but not planned
- **Validated**: validated by the boat owner
- **Planned**: planned by you
- **In Progress**: intervention started
- **Closed**: intervention finished
- **Cancelled**

## Documentation

You can follow interventions on each boat, and in particular, from the tabs appearing at the bottom:

- Add photos taken during the intervention
- Add / complete notes taken by mechanics during interventions
