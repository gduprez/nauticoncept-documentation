# My Account

![My Account](../../media/manuel-concessionnaires/nauticoncept_moncompte.png)

The My Account page displays your personal details and allows you to modify them.

To modify your last name, first name, address, and phone number, click on "Modify my personal information".

You can also modify your PIN code as well as your password.
