# Login

![Login Screen](../../media/manuel-concessionnaires/nauticoncept_double_login.png)

After launching the application, the home screen asks you to enter your email address and the password you received. After entering them, click on identification.

The next screen prompts you to choose a PIN code that you can use to open the application more quickly. The next time you use it, only the PIN code will be necessary. We advise you to enter a 4-digit PIN code.

> **Important:** It is nevertheless possible that after an application update you may be asked to enter your email address and password again. Please make sure to keep it safe.
