# Take a Photo

![Take a Photo](../../media/manuel-concessionnaires/nauticoncept_newphoto-1.png)

From the list of photos of your boat, simply click on the camera icon at the top right of your screen. A menu appears at the bottom offering you several options:

## Photo

This option will optimize the photo you take for display over the internet. Calculations (about 50%) of the image allows faster access to the photo. This option is very satisfactory for overview shots not requiring a high level of detail.

## HD Photo

This option will use the maximum capabilities of your phone in terms of resolution to obtain the sharpest photo possible. Loading the photo into the application will take longer, but will allow you to retrieve an excellent level of detail / definition. This option is particularly suitable for memorizing serial numbers on technical parts of the boat, documents or any text content.

## Photo from Gallery

This option allows you to store in the application, and therefore verify, a photo already present on your smartphone or tablet. In this case, the application directs you to your photo gallery and your internal memory.

> After importing or taking a photo, you will be able to access its properties to classify it (tags).
